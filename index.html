What's in the box?




The Ovaphone

An intuitive, easy to use device designed to make video calling more accessible to the elderly, the Ovaphone was developed to address common issues elderly people encounter when facing technology: video calls are made easier than ever by allowing calls to be made at the simple press of a button! When not in use, the Ovaphone's screen dims down to an unintrusive brightness, while staying connected to make sure family and friends are reachable at all times!


A stand

Holding your Ovaphone in place, the stand provided allows more freedom when calling and snuggly holds your device when not in use.


A power cable

With the power cable constantly plugged in, issues such as mislocating your Ovaphone or overlooking to charge it are a thing of the past!


A user guide

We've provided you with a user guide for all those frustrating questions standing between you and your first call.
